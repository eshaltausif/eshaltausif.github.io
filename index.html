My Schooling

I HAVE SURVIVED...

8 years of Primary School

I have been so far tortured and tormented in previous primary school years with... Math! The worst thing ever concocted in history so far. I tried my best to reason with my teachers to use something much more better called English, but nobody agrees. My favourite subject of all in school has always been and forever will be English. The worst: Math. My fellow peers understand my fear of math but not so much with the adults. My favourite pastime is reading. Books are the best invention made. I am normally found reading in the sunlight on a warm summer day or curled up in my bed reading on a crisp, wintry day. This nobody understands, not even some of my best friends who are quite concerned about why I love reading this much.
